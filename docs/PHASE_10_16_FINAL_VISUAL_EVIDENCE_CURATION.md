# Final Presentation Evidence Curation

## Purpose

This phase selects existing, fixed-reference figures and replays for later human
review and final-report assembly. It never reruns a mission, changes physical
parameters, builds a Word report, creates a delivery ZIP or makes a release
claim.

## Inclusion policy

- Selected sources must be `reference_*` artifacts only.
- Historical and Legacy animations are excluded.
- Every retained boundary or controlled-failure asset is explicitly classified.
- Selected figure SVG text is inspected for visible `Phase` or `Patch` labels.
- GIF and MP4 assets must meet documented readability, duration and resolution
  thresholds.

## Output boundary

The `outputs/presentation_evidence/` folder contains copied, source-qualified
assets for review. It does not replace the original evidence or broaden the
model validity boundary.
