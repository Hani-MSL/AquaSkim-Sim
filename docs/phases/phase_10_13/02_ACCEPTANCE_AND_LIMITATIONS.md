# Acceptance and limitations

Acceptance metrics are generated from logged RK4 state histories after the
manoeuvre onset and settling interval:

- p95 absolute cross-track error;
- final absolute cross-track error;
- p95 absolute heading error;
- mean ground speed;
- the measurable open-loop cross-current drift; and
- the maximum values over the documented 3 × 3 gain grid.

The study validates only the versioned low-current input with magnitude not
above 0.02 m/s. It is not an optimization, a global stability proof, an
open-water station-keeping claim or a substitute for physical trials.
